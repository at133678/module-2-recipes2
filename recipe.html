Neat Sloppy Joes

Click shere
Ingredients Directions
2 pounds lean ground beef Place ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook until evenly browened, stirring to crumble. I like to use 2 potato mashers to even out the clumps.
1/2 cup chopped onion and 1 cup of chopped celery Add onions and celery, cover the pan, and cook until tender and transparent, about 5 minutes. Drain off any grease.
1(10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato Stir the tomato soup (undiluted)
1/4 cup ketchup, 1 tablespoon white vinegar, 1/4 cup packed brown sugar, and 1 1/2 teaspoons Worcestershire Ketchup, vinegar, brown sugar, and Worcestershire sauce into the beef mixture.
1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder Season with salt and garlic powder. Heat to a simmer over low heat, and cook until thoroughly heated, stirring frequently to prevent it from burning on the bottom.
8 hambuger buns Spoon the hot beef mixture into buns, which may be toasted first, and serve.

French Toast

Click here
Ingredients Directions
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg 2 tablespoons sugar In a small bowl, combine cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar and set aside briefly.
4 tablespoons butter 4 eggs 1/4 cup milk 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 8 slices challah, brioche, or white bread 1/2 cup maple syrup, warmed In a 10-inch or 12-inch skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk together cinnamon mixture, eggs, milk, and vanilla and pour into a shallow container such as a pie plate. Dip bread in egg mixture.
